Ugali recipe
  • 3 cups of maize flour
  • 6 cups of water
  1. Put the six cups of water into a sufuria
  2. Heat the water until it starts boiling
  3. Add the 3 cups of maize flour into the boiling water
  4. Stir the mixture until it hardens
  5. Continue stirring for upto 10 mins
  6. when a smell of pop corn is produced the ugali is ready to be served.
